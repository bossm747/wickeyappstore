WAS SSO login?


WickeyAppStore apps use SSO (single sign on) for security and ease of use.

When you login it will email you a 6 digit token, which you then verify in the app.


Then, all other WickeyAppStore apps will *auto-magically be logged in.


*This only works on the same physical device
